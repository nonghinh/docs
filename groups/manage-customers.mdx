---
title: Add customers to a group
description: Assign customers to wholesale groups, understand the customer-tag bridge, and use a default group.
---

Once a group exists, populate it with customers. The app supports two paths: **manual assignment** in the admin, and **automatic assignment** when an application is approved.

## Add customers manually

1. Go to **Customers** in the left navigation.
2. Click **Add customer**.
3. In the search field, type a name or email. Results come from your live Shopify customer list.
4. Select one or more customers, choose the **target group**, then click **Add**.

The app:

1. Creates a `GroupCustomer` row linking the customer to the group.
2. Writes the tag `wholesale-{slug}` to the customer in Shopify.
3. The customer immediately starts seeing wholesale prices on the storefront and at checkout.

## Move a customer to a different group

1. From the **Customers** list, find the customer and click the row.
2. Select a new group from the dropdown.
3. Click **Save**.

The app removes the old `wholesale-*` tag and writes the new one.

## Remove a customer from wholesale

Set the customer's group to **None**. The app removes the `wholesale-*` tag and the customer reverts to retail pricing immediately.

## Customer tag bridge

Every wholesale customer carries a tag like `wholesale-silver`. This tag is what the Shopify checkout function reads to decide which group's pricing applies.

Two rules to keep in mind:

- **Don't edit `wholesale-*` tags directly in Shopify Admin.** The app and Shopify will get out of sync.
- **Don't reuse the prefix `wholesale-` for any other tag.** The app treats every tag with that prefix as a wholesale tag.

→ See [Key concepts](/getting-started/key-concepts) for the full discount-resolution flow.

## Default group

You can designate one group as the **default**. When set, customers approved through a registration form land in the default group unless the form specifies a different target.

To set a default group:

1. Go to **Settings → Shop settings**.
2. Under **Default group**, choose the group from the dropdown.
3. Click **Save**.

Setting a default does **not** retroactively assign existing customers — it only affects future approvals.

## What a customer sees

Once tagged, a wholesale customer sees:

- Wholesale prices on product pages (if the **Wholesale price** block is installed in your theme).
- Wholesale prices in the cart and at checkout — always, handled by the Shopify Function.
- A wholesale account dashboard at `/apps/wholesale/account`.
