---
title: Price rules
description: Override a group's baseline discount for individual products or variants.
---

A **price rule** sets a custom discount on a specific product or variant for one wholesale group. Use price rules when one or two products need a different rate from the group's baseline.

## When to use a price rule

| Situation | Use a price rule? |
| --- | --- |
| One premium product needs a smaller discount than the rest of the catalog | ✅ Yes |
| You want to reward bulk orders with deeper discounts | ❌ Use a [volume tier](/pricing/volume-tiers) instead |
| The whole group should get a flat discount | ❌ Use the **group baseline** instead |
| A specific variant (size or color) needs its own price | ✅ Yes — set a variant-level rule |

## Create a price rule

1. Go to **Pricing**, then click the group you want to add a rule to.
2. In the **Price rules** card, click **Add rule**.
3. Choose the scope:
   - **Product** — applies to every variant of the selected product.
   - **Variant** — applies to one specific variant only.
4. Use the product picker to select the product (or variant).
5. Choose the **discount type** (`percentage` or `fixed amount`) and enter the **value**.
6. Click **Save**.

The rule takes effect within seconds — both on product pages (via the theme block) and at checkout (via the Shopify Function).

## Resolution rules

When a cart line has both a product-level rule and a variant-level rule, **the variant rule wins**. This lets you set a default for a product and then override one specific variant.

Example, for the **Gold** group:

| Layer | Resulting discount on *T-shirt — Red / XL* |
| --- | --- |
| Group baseline | 25% |
| Product rule on *T-shirt* | 20% |
| Variant rule on *Red / XL* | 15% |

The cart line shows **15% off**. The variant rule overrides the product rule, which itself overrides the baseline.

If a volume tier also applies, the **lowest final price** still wins overall — see [Pricing](/pricing).

## Edit or delete a rule

- **Edit** — click the rule's row to change the discount type or value.
- **Delete** — click the trash icon. Deleting is immediate; the line falls back to the next layer (variant → product → group baseline).

## Bulk operations

To add price rules for many products at once, repeat the **Add rule** flow. The app does not currently support CSV import or bulk product selection. For very large catalogs, set a sensible group baseline and use price rules only for exceptions.

<Tip>
  Audit your price rules quarterly. As your catalog evolves, rules can pile up on products you no longer carry.
</Tip>
