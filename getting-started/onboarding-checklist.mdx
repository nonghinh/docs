The dashboard shows a **Setup checklist** the first time you open the app. Each step auto-completes as you take the action — except the theme extension step, which you confirm manually.

The checklist hides itself once all steps are done.

## The five steps

### 1. Create a wholesale group

Groups are the foundation of every other feature. A group defines a baseline discount (for example, *20% off*) and which customers belong to it.

→ [Create a wholesale group](/groups/create-a-group)

This step completes when at least one group exists.

### 2. Create a registration form

Forms let prospects apply to your wholesale program from the storefront. Submissions appear in the **Applications** inbox for you to review.

This step completes when at least one form exists.

### 3. Add a price rule or volume tier

Price rules override the group's baseline discount for specific products or variants. Volume tiers reward larger orders with deeper discounts.

→ [Price rules](/pricing/price-rules) · [Volume tiers](/pricing/volume-tiers)

This step completes when at least one price rule **or** volume tier exists.

### 4. Configure email sender settings

Set the **From** name and reply-to address for transactional emails (application received, approved, rejected, and so on). Without this, emails fall back to a generic sender.

This step completes when sender settings are saved.

### 5. Acknowledge the theme extension

The app ships a Theme App Extension that renders wholesale prices on product pages and embeds the registration form. You must add the block to your live theme yourself, then check the **Theme extension added** box on the dashboard.

This step is **manual** — the app cannot detect block placement automatically. Re-confirm after publishing a new theme.

## When the theme changes

If you publish a different theme, the app shows a *Theme changed — re-verify the block* banner for 14 days. The acknowledgement resets so you remember to add the block to the new theme.

<Tip>
  You can re-open the checklist at any time from the dashboard's **Setup guide** card if you need to re-acknowledge a step.
</Tip>
