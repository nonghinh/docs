2Boys Wholesale B2B Suite turns any Shopify store into a wholesale-ready storefront. Create custom price tiers, accept B2B applications, and apply discounts at checkout — without upgrading to Shopify Plus.

## What you'll do

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    Install from the Shopify App Store and grant the required scopes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the onboarding checklist">
    The dashboard walks you through the five setup steps every store needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first wholesale group">
    Define a tier (such as **Silver** or **VIP**) with a baseline discount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set product and volume pricing">
    Override prices per product or reward bulk orders with quantity breaks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish a registration form">
    Embed a B2B application form on your storefront so prospects can sign up.
  </Step>
</Steps>
